# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Singapore home buyers comparing BTO projects, grants, monthly affordability,
and payment timing. They are usually scanning numbers under uncertainty and need
the interface to make tradeoffs legible without turning the task into research.

## Product Purpose

Help buyers estimate affordability, understand grants and upfront payments, and
compare BTO launches using the generated RecordBTO dataset. Success means a user
can move from income and flat assumptions to a short, practical view of viable
projects and payment milestones.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, practical.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page heroes, glassmorphism, decorative gradients, oversized
metric cards, vague fintech copy, and dashboard visuals that obscure the actual
housing numbers.

## Design Principles

- Put decisions before decoration.
- Make dense housing data scan cleanly.
- Keep calculations visibly connected to their inputs.
- Prefer familiar controls and predictable layout.
- Treat missing scraped data honestly.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, visible focus states,
plain language, and reduced reliance on color alone for status.
